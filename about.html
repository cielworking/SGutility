ℹ️ About SG Alerts

SG Alerts is an independent Singapore information dashboard providing updates on checkpoint traffic, Singapore news, petrol prices, COE results, BTO launches and other useful daily information.

Our goal is to make important Singapore information easily accessible through a single platform.

Information displayed on this website is aggregated from publicly available sources and official government data feeds where available.

SG Alerts is not affiliated with any Singapore government agency.
