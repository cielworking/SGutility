FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is TeleX?

TeleX is an Android media browser and downloader for Telegram chats that your Telegram account can access.

Is TeleX a Telegram client?

No. TeleX is focused on browsing and managing media. It does not aim to replace Telegram messaging.

Where are downloads saved?

Files are saved to Downloads/TelegramDownloader on your device.

Why do I need to log in?

TeleX uses your Telegram login to access your chats, and your TeleX account to validate your license.

Can I transfer my license to another device?

Contact support. Device transfer depends on your license limit and current activation status.

Why does a link sometimes not open a specific media item?

Telegram may limit direct message lookup in some private groups or topics. TeleX shows the most specific error available from TDLib.
